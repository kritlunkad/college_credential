The system will perform the following checks:

  • 🔏 Issuer signature — Is the credential signed by a trusted institution?
  • 🛡️ Data integrity — Has the credential been tampered with?
  • ⏱️ Expiry check — Is the credential still valid?
  • ⛔ Revocation check — Has the issuer revoked the credential?
  • 🔄 Replay detection — Has this presentation been used before?
  • ⛓️ Blockchain anchor — Is the presentation hash anchored on-chain?
  • 👥 Role separation — Are issuer and student different wallets?
  • 🧮 ZKP proofs — Are the range claims mathematically valid?
