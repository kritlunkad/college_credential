How it works

01

College Issues

The university digitally signs academic credentials with their ECDSA private key. The public key is embedded for self-contained verification.

02

Student Claims

Credentials live in the student's wallet — not in a central database. Students claim using their enrollment ID and connect their wallet.

03

Selectively Share

Toggle switches control which fields to reveal. Generate a verifiable presentation with optional ZKP range proofs for sensitive values.

04

Anyone Verifies

Employers or scholarship boards enter a code or scan QR. The system checks the digital signature and validates ZKP proofs — instant, mathematical trust.
